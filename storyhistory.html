In A.D. 675, April. Tang Dynasty. Outside of the Crown Prince Hong’s Hebi Palace, flowers flourish, another spring finally came. Inside the Hebi Palace, everyone fells in danger. Prince Hong sent people to assassinate the courtier Wen Xueming who is the Empress’s confidant, and his deeds were revealed. The Empress Consort Wu Zetian has considerable political power. After Emperor Gaozong's debilitating stroke in A.D. 660, Wu Zetian became administrator of the court, a position equal to the emperor. Empress Wu and Crown Prince Hong is going to have a drinking game tonight.
